**Bull Rebuttal:**

1. **It's already declining:** 96% → ~92% as Other Revenue scales

3. **Reserve yield is DURABLE:** Circle earns yield whether USDC sits idle or transacts. Volume growth compounds both reserve income AND transaction fees.
4. **Fed rate trajectory is overblown:**
   - Current SOFR: ~4.26%
   - Even at 3.0% rates, reserve income = ~$1.3B (still profitable)
   - Meanwhile, USDC circulation growing 40%+ annually
   - **Volume growth offsets rate compression**

**Math:** If circulation doubles to $160B and rates fall to 3.0%, reserve income = $160B × 3.0% = $4.8B (vs. $1.74B today)

### Bear Concern #2: "USDT Compliance Kills the Moat"
**Bull Rebuttal:**

1. **Audit ≠ Compliance:** Tether announced a Big Four audit, but:
   - No timeline disclosed
   - Auditor not named (likely takes 12-18 months)
   - Full audit ≠ GENIUS Act compliance (operational changes needed)
   - Historical illicit usage patterns create regulatory overhang

2. **Circle has 3+ years head start** on institutional relationships:
   - Deloitte annual audits since 2018
   - Monthly attestations for years
   - Relationships with JPMorgan, Visa, Mastercard already live

3. **William Blair analysts note:** Tether still faces "potential U.S. regulatory scrutiny over illicit USDT usage patterns" — an audit doesn't erase history

4. **USDT's market share is concentrated in offshore/DeFi** where compliance matters less. The high-value institutional market (banks, Fortune 500, AI agents) is USDC's to lose.

5. **Even if USDT becomes compliant:**
   - Rising tide lifts all boats — stablecoin TAM grows
