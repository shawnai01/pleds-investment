## §8 Bull/Bear Debate on Valuation

### 🐻 Bear Case: Why TOST Could Be Overvalued

**1. It's a Payments Company, Not SaaS**
- 68% of revenue from payments processing
- True SaaS is only 29% of revenue
- Payments businesses trade at 10-12x EBITDA, not 22x
- GPN at 10x, FISV at 7x, Shift4 at 10x → TOST at 22x is stretched

**2. SBC Dilution Is Real**
- $600M+ annual SBC = ~4% dilution per year
- $633M EBITDA looks great until you subtract $600M SBC
- GAAP earnings ($342M) tell a different story
- SBC as % of revenue (10%+) is still high vs mature peers

**3. Restaurant TAM Is Limited**
- 860k restaurants in US, TOST has 164k (19% penetration)
- Path to 300k+ requires SMB churn victories (hard)
- International expansion is expensive and unproven
- Already the dominant player — where's the growth?

**4. Competition Is Intensifying**
- Square for Restaurants improving rapidly
- Clover (Fiserv) gaining share
- Lightspeed, SpotOn, TouchBistro fighting for same pie
- Pricing pressure inevitable

**5. Macro Sensitivity**
- Restaurant spend correlates with consumer health
- Recession = closures + reduced GPV
- High beta (1.95) = violent downside in risk-off

**Bear Fair Value: $22-26/share (at current price, fully valued)**

---

### 🐂 Bull Case: Why TOST Could Be Worth $50+

**1. It's a Platform, Not Just Payments**
- Network effects: suppliers, guests, delivery integrate
- Data moat: knows every menu item, pricing trend, labor cost
- Payment processing is the wedge, but SaaS is the prize
- 80% SaaS gross margins vs 35% payments
- SaaS growing 28% and accelerating ARPU

**2. The Flywheel Is Working**
- Each new module increases ARPU
- Payroll, scheduling, inventory, marketing all upselling
- Net retention 109% — customers spending more each year
- Record 30k location adds in FY25 — growth not slowing

**3. FCF Inflection Is Happening**
- FCF nearly doubled to $608M in FY25
- FCF margin expanding from 7% → 10% → 15%+
- SBC declining as % of revenue
- Buybacks now returning capital

**4. International Is Free Option Value**
- UK and Canada launches in progress
- $0 revenue from international currently
- Restaurant tech globally fragmented
- Replicating US playbook = TAM expansion

**5. Acquisition Currency**
- Strategic acquirer would pay 30-50% premium
- Unique asset: unified restaurant OS
- Fiserv, Block, Oracle all logical buyers
- Floor under stock via M&A optionality

**Bull Fair Value: $50-60/share**
