### Argument 2: "A2A tailwind — stablecoins are the future of payments!"

**Reality: Circle doesn't monetize velocity**

This is the biggest misconception in crypto finance.

When money moves FAST (A2A, instant settlement):
- Less time parked in reserves
- Less yield generated
- Transaction fees are ~0% (Circle charges nothing meaningful)

**Real example:**
- Corporate treasury holds $10M in USDC for 30 days → Circle earns ~$35K in reserve income
- Corporate treasury uses USDC for instant A2A payments, average balance $500K → Circle earns ~$1.75K

**A2A makes USDC more useful but Circle less profitable.**

The companies that benefit from A2A are:
- **Payment processors** (Stripe, Square) — fee on transactions
- **Wallets** (Coinbase, MetaMask) — fee on swaps
- **NOT stablecoin issuers** — they're just the rails
